## Working Document Structure

When building a full trip plan, organize as:

1. **Trip Overview** — dates, travelers, destinations, total budget
2. **Transportation** — all flights, trains, transfers with confirmation numbers and times
3. **Accommodation** — addresses, check-in/out times, contact info
4. **Day-by-Day Itinerary** — the detailed plan
5. **Restaurant List** — organized by city and meal, with reservation status
6. **Budget Tracker** — what's booked/paid vs. estimated remaining
7. **Book Ahead Checklist** — with deadlines and booking links
8. **Packing List** — tailored to destinations and activities
9. **Emergency Info** — embassy contacts, insurance info, emergency numbers

## Accommodation Red Flags Reference

| Word | Actual Meaning |
|------|---------------|
| "Cozy" | Tiny |
| "Charming" | Old |
| "Authentic" | No AC |
| "Steps from the beach" | 15-minute walk |
| "Great location" | Vague — verify with specific address |
| All 5-star reviews, only 3-5 reviews | Too few data points |

## Pre-Trip Checklist Template

### Documents & Admin
- [ ] Passport valid for 6+ months beyond return date
- [ ] Visa requirements checked and applications submitted
- [ ] Travel insurance purchased
- [ ] Copies of passport, insurance, bookings (digital + physical)
- [ ] Bank/credit card companies notified of travel dates
- [ ] Emergency contacts list

### Bookings to Confirm
- [ ] Flights — confirmation numbers, check-in reminders set
- [ ] Accommodation — addresses, check-in procedures, contact info
- [ ] Must-book activities and reservations
- [ ] Airport transfers if pre-arranged
- [ ] Car rental if applicable (international driving permit if needed)

### Money
- [ ] Primary payment method (no foreign transaction fees)
- [ ] Backup payment method
- [ ] Small amount of local currency for arrival
- [ ] Daily budget set

### Tech & Connectivity
- [ ] Phone plan: eSIM, local SIM, or international plan activated
- [ ] Offline maps downloaded (Google Maps or Maps.me)
- [ ] Translation app with offline language packs
- [ ] Key apps installed (transit, ride-sharing, accommodation)
- [ ] Power adapter for destination country

### Health
- [ ] Required/recommended vaccinations
- [ ] Prescription medications with extra supply
- [ ] Basic first aid / travel health kit
- [ ] Health/travel insurance card accessible

### Packing
- [ ] Luggage packed and under weight limit
- [ ] Carry-on essentials: documents, medications, change of clothes, chargers
- [ ] Weather-appropriate clothing for destination
- [ ] Comfortable walking shoes
- [ ] Any required clothing (dress codes for religious sites, etc.)
